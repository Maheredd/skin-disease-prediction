About This Website

This web application uses Deep Learning, specifically Convolutional Neural Networks (CNNs), to classify skin images into one of eight skin diseases:

  • Cellulitis
  • Impetigo
  • Athlete-Foot
  • Nail-Fungus
  • Ringworm
  • Cutaneous-larva-migrans
  • Chickenpox
  • Shingles
